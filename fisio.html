Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy Treatment includes many techniques such as massage, manipulation and neuromuscular release therapy. Every patient Bruna treats and every injury/condition that is seen is treated on an individual case-by-case basis. Therefore Bruna may also utilise the likes of acupuncture/dry needling, exercise therapy, laser therapy and ultrasound therapy if it is clinically indicated. All of this will be discussed with you and explained to you as part of your treatment plan. Home Service

Bruna provides a physiotherapy home visit service that is ideally suited to patients who are unable, for whatever reason, to make it into her own physiotherapy clinic. Bruna will handle Home Visits on a case by case basis by appointment only with each visit lasting for 60 minutes. This is a specialist physiotherapy home visit service dedicated to providing high quality domiciliary based care in the Mato Gorsso du Sol region
